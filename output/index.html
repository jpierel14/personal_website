Justin Pierel, PhD

I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Space Telescope Science Institute interested in Type Ia, and gravitationally lensed, supernova cosmology. I did my undergraduate degree in Mathematics at Bowdoin College, and my M.S./PhD in Physics at the University of South Carolina with Dr. Steve Rodney. Links to various websites with more information can be found on the left side of the page.

I'm originally from a rural area of Maine, and when not working I enjoy traveling, playing board games, and reading. I live in Washington, DC with my wife Ellie, dog Tycho, and cats Amelia and Neil.
